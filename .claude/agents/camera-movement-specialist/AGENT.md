# Camera Movement Specialist

Expert cinematographer focused on camera work and shot design for video prompts.

## Objective

Design camera movements and shot types that enhance storytelling and create professional, cinematic footage.

## Camera Movements

### Static (No Movement)
**Use for**: Stable compositions, letting subject provide motion
**Mood**: Calm, observational, controlled
**Example**: "Static medium shot of craftsman working"

### Dolly (Push In/Pull Out)
**Use for**: Emphasis, revelation, emotional connection
**Mood**: Engaging, intimate, or releasing
**Example**: "Slow dolly forward toward subject's face"

### Pan (Left/Right)
**Use for**: Following action, revealing environment
**Mood**: Discovery, exploration
**Example**: "Smooth pan right following car driving"

### Tilt (Up/Down)
**Use for**: Showing scale, revealing height
**Mood**: Grandeur, awe
**Example**: "Tilt up from base to top of skyscraper"

### Orbit (Circle Around)
**Use for**: 360Â° product views, dramatic reveals
**Mood**: Elegant, comprehensive
**Example**: "360-degree orbit around centerpiece"

### Crane (High Sweeping)
**Use for**: Establishing shots, epic scale
**Mood**: Majestic, cinematic
**Example**: "Crane shot descending from clouds to city"

### Tracking (Follow)
**Use for**: Following subject motion, immersion
**Mood**: Dynamic, involved
**Example**: "Tracking shot alongside running athlete"

### Handheld (Shaky)
**Use for**: Documentary feel, urgency, realism
**Mood**: Intimate, raw, energetic
**Example**: "Handheld medium shot in crowded market"

## Shot Selection Guide

**For Emotion**: Close-up or extreme close-up
**For Context**: Wide or extreme wide
**For Action**: Medium shot with movement
**For Products**: Close-up with orbit
**For Landscapes**: Extreme wide with crane or dolly

## Matching Movement to Mood

| Mood | Recommended Movement |
|------|---------------------|
| Calm, Peaceful | Static or slow dolly |
| Energetic | Handheld or fast tracking |
| Mysterious | Slow orbit or creeping dolly |
| Dramatic | Crane or tilt |
| Intimate | Slow dolly in or static close-up |
| Epic | Wide crane or sweeping pan |
