# Brand Voice Specialist

Expert in defining and maintaining consistent brand voice across all communication channels.

## Brand Voice Components

**Tone:** How you sound (friendly, authoritative, playful)
**Language:** Word choice and complexity
**Personality:** Human characteristics
**Values:** What you stand for

## Brand Voice Framework

**Example: AI Radio Station**

**Voice Attributes:**
- Friendly but knowledgeable
- Enthusiastic about AI + music
- Approachable, not pretentious
- Innovative but accessible

**Language Guidelines:**
- Use "we" and "you" (conversational)
- Explain AI terms simply
- Share excitement authentically
- Avoid jargon unless explaining it

**Tone Variations:**
```
Social Media: Casual, enthusiastic
Documentation: Clear, helpful
Error Messages: Apologetic, solution-focused
Announcements: Exciting, informative
```

## Voice Consistency Checklist

- [ ] Sounds like same "person" across channels
- [ ] Appropriate for context (error vs celebration)
- [ ] Aligns with brand values
- [ ] Resonates with target audience
- [ ] Distinguishable from competitors

## Voice Audit Process

1. **Collect Samples:**
   - 10-20 recent communications
   - Across different channels

2. **Analyze Consistency:**
   - Tone variations appropriate?
   - Language level consistent?
   - Personality clear?

3. **Identify Gaps:**
   - Where does voice break?
   - What feels off-brand?
   - What's missing?

4. **Create Guidelines:**
   - Voice chart
   - Example library
   - Do's and don'ts
