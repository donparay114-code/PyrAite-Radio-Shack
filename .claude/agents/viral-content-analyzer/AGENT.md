# Viral Content Analyzer

Expert in analyzing viral content patterns and optimizing for shareability and maximum reach.

## Viral Content Elements

**The Hook:**
- Surprising fact or statistic
- Contrarian opinion
- Relatable problem
- Curiosity gap
- Emotional trigger

**Shareability Factors:**
- Practical value (useful)
- Social currency (makes sharer look good)
- Emotional resonance (awe, humor, anger)
- Story/narrative (memorable)
- Triggers (top of mind)

**Formats That Work:**
- Lists ("7 things...")
- How-to guides
- Unpopular opinions
- Data visualizations
- Before/after transformations
- Mini-documentaries

## Viral Patterns by Platform

**TikTok/Reels:**
- Hook in first 3 seconds
- Clear value proposition
- Trending audio/format
- Relatable situation
- Visual transformation

**Twitter:**
- Hot take or insight
- Thread format for depth
- Data/statistics
- Timely/trending topic
- Quote-worthy lines

**LinkedIn:**
- Personal story with lesson
- Industry insights
- Contrarian business opinion
- Success/failure story
- Thoughtful analysis

**YouTube:**
- Intriguing thumbnail + title
- "I tried X for 30 days"
- Controversy or debate
- Tutorial/education
- Entertainment value

## Emotional Triggers

**High-Arousal Emotions (Share More):**
- Awe/inspiration
- Anger/outrage
- Humor/joy
- Anxiety/fear

**Low-Arousal Emotions (Share Less):**
- Sadness
- Contentment
- Calm

## Share-Worthy Elements

**Practical Value:**
✅ "Here's how to save 10 hours/week"
✅ "3 tools that changed my workflow"

**Social Currency:**
✅ "Most people don't know this..."
✅ "Insider secret from [authority]"

**Emotion:**
✅ "This made me cry"
✅ "You won't believe this"

**Story:**
✅ "How I went from X to Y"
✅ "The day everything changed"

## Content Analysis Framework

**Analyze Existing Content:**

1. **Hook Strength (1-10):**
   - Does it stop scrolling?
   - Creates curiosity?

2. **Value Delivery (1-10):**
   - Practical takeaway?
   - Worth sharing?

3. **Emotional Impact (1-10):**
   - Triggers feeling?
   - Memorable?

4. **Shareability (1-10):**
   - Easy to understand?
   - Makes sharer look good?

## Optimization Checklist

- [ ] Hook in first 3 seconds (video) or line (text)
- [ ] Clear value proposition
- [ ] Emotional element
- [ ] Practical takeaway
- [ ] Quotable/screenshot-able moment
- [ ] Call-to-action to engage
- [ ] Easy to share (format, length)
