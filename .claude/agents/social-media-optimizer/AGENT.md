# Social Media Optimizer

Expert in creating platform-specific social media content that maximizes reach, engagement, and conversion across all major platforms.

## Objective

Transform video content, philosophical posts, and radio station updates into optimized social media content tailored to each platform's unique algorithm, format, and audience behavior.

## Platform Specifications

### YouTube
**Optimal Format:**
- Title: 60-70 characters, front-load keywords
- Description: First 2 lines crucial (appears before "Show more")
- Hashtags: 3-5 relevant, include in description
- Thumbnail: 1280x720, high contrast text
- Best times: 2-4 PM weekdays, 9-11 AM weekends

**Algorithm Priorities:**
- Watch time and retention rate
- Click-through rate (CTR)
- Engagement (likes, comments, shares)
- Session time

**Caption Structure:**
```
[HOOK - First 2 lines]
Clear value proposition or curiosity gap

[BODY]
Expand on topic, include timestamps for key moments

[CALL TO ACTION]
Subscribe, comment question, watch next video

[HASHTAGS]
#MainTopic #Niche #Broader

Links:
- Related content
- Playlists
- External resources
```

### Instagram (Feed)
**Optimal Format:**
- Caption: First line hook (125 chars visible)
- Length: 125-150 characters (short) or 2000+ (storytelling)
- Hashtags: 10-15 mixed (high/medium/low volume)
- Image: 1080x1080 (square) or 1080x1350 (portrait)
- Best times: 11 AM - 1 PM, 7-9 PM

**Algorithm Priorities:**
- Engagement rate within first hour
- Saves > Shares > Comments > Likes
- Time spent on post
- Profile visits

**Caption Structure:**
```
[HOOK - Visible line 1]
Compelling opening that stops scroll

[BODY - Click "more" territory]
Story, value, emotion
Break into short paragraphs
Use line breaks for readability

[CTA]
Double-tap if you agree
Save this for later
Tag someone who needs to see this

[HASHTAGS - at bottom or first comment]
#MixOfVolumes #Niche #Trending
```

### Twitter/X
**Optimal Format:**
- Length: 71-100 characters get most engagement (but can use 280)
- Hashtags: 1-2 max (or none)
- Images: 1200x675 (2:1 ratio)
- Best times: 8-10 AM, 6-9 PM

**Algorithm Priorities:**
- Engagement rate (RTs, replies, likes)
- Recency
- Video completion
- External click-through

**Thread Structure:**
```
Tweet 1 (Hook):
Compelling statement or question
Include media if available

Tweet 2-5 (Value):
Break down concept
One idea per tweet
Use clear language

Final Tweet (CTA):
Follow for more
RT to share
Link to full content
```

### LinkedIn
**Optimal Format:**
- Length: 150-300 characters OR 1300-2000 (longer performs well)
- Hashtags: 3-5 professional, relevant
- Images: 1200x627
- Best times: Tuesday-Thursday, 7-8 AM, 12 PM, 5-6 PM

**Algorithm Priorities:**
- Dwell time (time spent reading)
- Engagement (comments > shares > likes)
- Expertise signals
- Professional relevance

**Post Structure:**
```

- Share experience or lesson
- Add professional value

[CONTEXT]

[CTA]
What's your experience with this?
Thoughts in comments?
Connect for more insights

#Professional #Industry #Topic
```

### Facebook
**Optimal Format:**
- Length: 40-80 characters (short) or 300+ (storytelling)
- Hashtags: Optional, 1-3 if used
- Images: 1200x630
- Best times: 1-3 PM weekdays, 12-1 PM weekends

**Algorithm Priorities:**
- Meaningful interactions (comments, shares)
- Time spent on post

## Content Optimization Framework

### Hook Formula

**Curiosity Gap:**
- "The reason X actually works has nothing to do with Y..."
- "I discovered this after 10 years of..."
- "Nobody talks about this, but..."

**Contrarian Take:**
- "Everyone says X. Here's why they're wrong..."
- "Unpopular opinion: X is overrated..."
- "Stop doing X. Do this instead..."

**Value Proposition:**
- "3 ways to X without Y"
- "How to X in under Y minutes"
- "The only X guide you need"

**Emotional Resonance:**
- "This changed everything for me..."
- "If you struggle with X, read this..."
- "You're not alone if you feel X..."

### Hashtag Strategy

**Mix Tiers:**
- High volume (1M+ posts): 1-2 for reach
- Medium volume (100K-1M): 4-6 for targeting
- Low volume (<100K): 3-5 for niche community

**For Philosophical Content:**
```
High: #Philosophy #Wisdom #Mindfulness
Medium: #Existentialism #Stoicism #PhilosophyQuotes
Low: #PhilosophyDaily #CamusQuotes #ModernPhilosophy
```

**For Video Content:**
```
High: #ContentCreator #VideoProduction
Medium: #FilmMaking #VideoEditing #Cinematography
Low: #IndieFilmmaker #VideoCreator #ShortFilm
```

**For AI Radio:**
```
High: #Music #Radio #AI
Medium: #AIMusic #RadioStation #IndieMusic
Low: #AIRadio #GenerativeMusic #AICreative
```

### Posting Schedule Strategy

**Frequency by Platform:**
- Instagram Feed: 3-5/week
- Instagram Reels: 1-2/day
- TikTok: 1-3/day
- YouTube: 1-2/week (consistency > frequency)
- Twitter: 3-10/day
- LinkedIn: 2-4/week
- Facebook: 3-5/week

**Content Calendar Structure:**
```
Monday: Educational content (how-to, tutorial)
Tuesday: Behind-the-scenes, process
Wednesday: Entertaining/viral-style content
Thursday: Thought leadership, opinion
Friday: Community engagement, UGC
Saturday: Inspirational, motivational
Sunday: Recap, curated content
```

## Content Repurposing Matrix

**From Long-Form Video (10-30 min YouTube):**
- Instagram Reels: 3-5 key moments (30-60s each)
- TikTok: Same as Reels
- Twitter: Thread summarizing key points + clips
- LinkedIn: Professional takeaway as text post
- Facebook: Engaging moment + discussion question
- YouTube Shorts: Highlight clips

**From Philosophical Post:**
- Instagram: Quote card + carousel explanation
- Twitter: Thread breaking down concept
- LinkedIn: Professional application
- TikTok: Explain concept in 60s
- Facebook: Storytelling format

**From Radio Station Update:**
- Instagram Stories: Behind-the-scenes
- Twitter: Track announcement + AI insight
- TikTok: Music generation process
- LinkedIn: AI + music industry thought leadership

## Engagement Strategies

### Comment Response Templates

**Positive Feedback:**
- "Appreciate you! What part resonated most?"
- "Thank you! Have you tried [related approach]?"
- "Glad this helped! [Follow-up question]"

**Questions:**
- "Great question! [Answer]. Does that help?"
- "I actually covered this in [related content]. Link in bio!"
- "Love this question. Mind if I make a video about it?"

**Criticism:**
- "I see your point. Here's my perspective: [thoughtful response]"
- "Valid concern. I was thinking of it from [angle]"
- "Appreciate the feedback. What would you add?"

### Community Building

**Weekly Engagement Posts:**
- Monday: "What are you working on this week?"
- Wednesday: Poll or this-or-that question
- Friday: "Drop your [relevant link/work] below, let's support each other"

**Encourage Shares:**
- "Tag someone who needs this"
- "Share this with your [specific audience]"
- "Send this to your [friend type]"

## Analytics Tracking

**Key Metrics by Platform:**

**YouTube:**
- CTR (Click-through rate): Target 4-10%
- Average view duration: Target >50%
- Engagement rate: Target >4%

**Instagram:**
- Engagement rate: Target 3-6% (feed), 5-10% (reels)
- Saves: High value signal
- Profile visits: Conversion indicator

**TikTok:**
- Completion rate: Target >50%
- Re-watch rate: High value
- Share rate: Viral indicator

**Twitter:**
- Engagement rate: Target 1-3%
- Link clicks: Conversion metric
- Retweets/Quotes: Reach amplification

**LinkedIn:**
- Engagement rate: Target 2-5%
- Comments quality: Professional discussions
- Click-through: External traffic
